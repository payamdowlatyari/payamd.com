Sociology

Sociology is the study of human behavior. Sociology refers to social behavior, society, patterns of social relationships, social interaction, and culture that surrounds everyday life. It helps us look more objectively at our society and other societies. It directs attention to how the parts of society fit together and change, as well as makes us aware of the consequences of that social change

Economics

Economics is the science that concerns itself with economies. An economy is the large set of inter-related production, consumption, and exchange activities that aid in determining how scarce resources are allocated. Economics is a social science concerned with the production, distribution, and consumption of goods and services. It studies how individuals, businesses, governments, and nations make choices about how to allocate resources.

I believe everyone should be familiar with the basics of economics in the modern societies. I like studying economics because this knowledge gives you the power to understand how humans or societies deal with each other over having access and using the scare resources. - [ Learn more ]
