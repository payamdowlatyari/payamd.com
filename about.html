Who I am

My name is Payam Dowlatyari. I am a software engineer, UX designer, hobbyist photographer, and blogger.
I graduated from UC Irvine with an undergraduate degree in Software Engineering and obtained a postgraduate certificate in Full Stack Web Development from Caltech.
I have designed, implemented, tested, deployed, and maintained web applications since 2019 as a freelancer and conversational AI user interfaces since 2020 at Amplify.ai as a full-time engineer. In recent years, I worked on many projects while contributing to research and open-source software.
Also, I'm interested in reading about various topics such as psychology, sociology, philosophy, and history. I enjoy walking and taking photos every day.
